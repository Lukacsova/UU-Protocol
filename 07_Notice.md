# UU Protocol Notice

The UU Protocol is a **privacy-preserving, neutral, and permissionless infrastructure** introducing **Human-Origin Proofs (HOPs), Proof-of-Humanity (POH), and Proof-of-Identity (POI)**.

## Important Security & Use Notice

- HOPs, POH, and POI are **cryptographically uncorruptible and immutable**.  
- These primitives **cannot be altered or deployed outside their intended design**.  

> **Warning:** Any modification, misuse, or integration outside the protocol’s intended design could enable:
>
> - Uncontrollable tracking  
> - HOPs manipulation  
> - Behavioral and identity data harvesting  
> - Aggressive misuse of the novel HOP marketplace  
> - Exploitation via the HOP delivery system  

- Unauthorized changes or deployment may compromise **privacy, neutrality, and public-benefit principles** and violate protocol invariants.  
- Use of protocol materials **without authorization from UU** is strictly prohibited for commercial or large-scale applications.

## Reporting Security Concerns

To report security issues or concerns, contact the stewards of the UU Protocol:  

**Uniting Users (UU)**  
**Email:** founder@uu-trust.org  

The protocol is currently in **pre-deployment phase**, and all integrations are evaluated for **mission-alignment, privacy preservation, and neutrality** before any deployment.
