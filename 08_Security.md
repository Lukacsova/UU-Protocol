# UU Protocol Security Policy

## Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you identify potential security issues in the UU Protocol:

- Contact the stewards of the UU Protocol, **Uniting Users**:  
  **founder@uu-trust.org**  

## Security Principles

- Protocol primitives are cryptographically uncorruptible.
- HOPs, POH, and POI operate without identity or personal data storage.
- Any alterations outside stewardship could compromise privacy and trust.
- Security reviews and audits are encouraged before pre-deployment or early integrations.
