# UU Protocol Invariants

## Core Principles

1. Human-Origin Proofs (HOPs), Proof-of-Humanity (POH), and Proof-of-Identity (POI) must operate **without identity databases or identity systems**.
2. Zero-storage architecture: No personal data, behavior, or identifying information is stored.
3. Privacy-preserving by design: Participation cannot be traced, tracked, or correlated.
4. Neutral infrastructure: The protocol cannot be controlled or biased by any party.
5. Permissionless: Any user can generate HOPs freely, without cost or approval.
6. Immutable primitives: HOPs, POH, and POI are cryptographically uncorruptible. Alterations or misused deployments are prohibited.
7. Stewardship protects integrity: Uniting Users stewards the protocol without centralized control.
