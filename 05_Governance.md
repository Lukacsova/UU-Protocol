# UU Protocol Governance

## Overview

The UU Protocol introduces **proof of human origin** and is designed to preserve:

- protocol neutrality
- privacy protection
- resistance to surveillance
- prevention of behavior exploitation
- global accessibility

---

## Governance Philosophy

**Protocol Principles → Ecosystem → Stewards**

- Protocol invariants are the highest authority.
- No steward or governance mechanism may override core principles.

---

## Current Phase

- Pre-deployment phase
- Architecture finalization
- Security and cryptographic review
- Long-horizon partner alignment
- Governance structures preparing for long-term stewardship

---

## Protocol Creator

**Uniting Users (UU)**: initial creator and steward.  
Responsibilities:

- maintaining protocol integrity
- coordinating ecosystem development
- protecting invariants

Stewardship does not override protocol invariants.

---

## Stewardship Model

- Neutral infrastructure
- Stewards coordinate responsible development
- Support ecosystem growth
- Encourage independent review
- Protocol remains **permissionless**

---

## Ecosystem Participation

Participants include developers, researchers, civil society, academic institutions, and platforms.  
All participation must respect protocol invariants.

---

## Economic and Infrastructure Separation

- Economic ecosystem and revenue are separate from protocol governance.
- Protocol decisions remain neutral, privacy-preserving, and free from commercial influence.

---

## Future Governance Evolution

- Governance may evolve to include advisory groups, community representatives, and decentralized mechanisms.
- Any new governance must respect the **protocol invariants**.
