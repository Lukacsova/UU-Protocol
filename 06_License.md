## Purpose

The UU Protocol introduces **proof of human origin**, a foundational internet primitive, while ensuring:

- **Zero-storage architecture**  
- **Privacy-by-design**  
- **Neutral, permissionless infrastructure**  
- **Immutable core primitives** (HOPs, POH, POI)

Use and generation of **HOPs for individual users is always free**.  

> **Important Note on IP Protection:**  
> The core HOP algorithm and its marketplace design are **not fully open-source**.  
> This restriction prevents misuse: if altered or deployed outside intended design, the mechanisms could enable **aggressive data collection, identity tracking, and behavioral profiling**.  
> IP protection ensures responsible ecosystem growth while preserving **privacy, neutrality, and public-good alignment**.

This license governs the **pre-deployment phase**, stewarded by UU, and remains in effect **until the protocol transitions into a fully automated, self-sustaining stewardship model managed by the UU Protocol itself**, without ongoing human oversight.

## Permitted Uses

- Independent research, academic study, and security audits  
- Public discussion, citation, and educational use  
- Non-commercial experimentation **without deploying protected algorithmic mechanisms**  
- Permissionless generation of HOPs by end users  

## Mission-Aligned Integrations

Integration with platforms, services, or commercial products is only permitted **with explicit written authorization from UU**, and must:

- Preserve privacy-by-design and zero-storage architecture  
- Maintain protocol neutrality and public-benefit alignment  
- Respect immutable HOP, POH, and POI primitives  
- Align with responsible, long-term ecosystem development

> **No integration may compromise protocol invariants or exploit the HOP algorithm**.

## Prohibited Uses

- Unauthorized deployment of protocol algorithms or infrastructure in commercial systems  
- Alteration, replication, or misappropriation of core protocol primitives  
- Integration into surveillance, tracking, or large-scale behavioral data collection platforms  
- Any use violating the UU Protocol invariants  

## Stewardship & Transition

- **Pre-deployment phase:** UU stewards the protocol to ensure alignment with privacy, neutrality, and public-good objectives.  
- **Transition to self-sustaining protocol stewardship:** The UU Protocol is designed to operate **autonomously**, with core mechanisms functioning without ongoing human oversight.  
- Stewardship during pre-deployment ensures **responsible ecosystem growth**, transparency, and adherence to protocol invariants.  
- UU retains stewardship authority only to protect **mission alignment and core protocol integrity** until self-sustaining governance is fully operational.
